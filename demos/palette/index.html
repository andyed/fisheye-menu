Flat-list Demo

148-Palette Picker

A Furnas focus+context lens applied to a flat list of 148 hand-crafted color palettes. The picker compresses the full list into a single viewport-tall column: items near the pointer expand to readable size, items far away compress toward a hard 4 px floor so distant entries remain clickable. From Andy Edmonds' Clemson MS research on dynamic target sizing in hierarchical menus.

Unlike the cascading-menu demo in index.html, this surface is one-dimensional — closer to the original 1986 Furnas formulation than to flyout navigation. The cursor-to-focus mapping uses a precomputeFisheyeFocusMap() table built once on open, so the focused row sits directly under the pointer even at the edges where Gaussian truncation would otherwise drift the alignment.
