Fisheye Flyout Menu

Luxuriously usable cascading menus. Items dynamically resize based on mouse proximity, applying Fitts's Law to reduce target acquisition time. Travel to flyout menus is enabled with a steering corridor computed with trigonometry. Mouse targets are carefully preserved even as the menu animates.

Try the menus above. Taxonomies from biology, chemistry, typography, Dewey Decimal, and Psychodeli+ color palettes.
