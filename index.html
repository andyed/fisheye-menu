Fisheye Flyout Menu

A Fitts's law optimization for cascading menus. Items closer to the mouse get taller targets; items farther away compress. The key constraint: the boundary between the current item and the next item below never moves toward the mouse — the goalpost stays put.

Flyout submenus inherit the same behavior and maintain a direct steering path from the parent item, eliminating the classic "diagonal problem" of cascading menus.
